About Project

Problem:

Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) is a serious complication of diabetes that affects the retina. Early detection is critical to prevent vision loss and blindness.

Solution:

This project uses Deep Learning with Xception CNN architecture to analyze fundus images and classify diabetic retinopathy stages efficiently and accurately.
